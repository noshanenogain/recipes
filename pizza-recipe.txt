Ingredients:
 - 1 pizza crust
 - 1 1 cup pizza sauce
 - 1 package of pepperoni
 - 1 package of Canadian bacon
 - 1 1 cup shredded cheese

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
 
 2. Spread sauce evenly on top of the pizza crust. Next place pepperoni and Canadian bacon spread out over the sauce.
	Once you have the desired amount of toppings, add the cup of cheese by sprinkling it on top. Try to spread out all ingredients.
 
 3. Bake roughly 15 minutes or until the toppings are cooked and the crust is at desired crispiness.
